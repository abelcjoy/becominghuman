About the Mission

CFH (Clarity for Humans) is a digital laboratory dedicated to the science of behavioral recovery. Our mission is to provide evidence-based insights and real-time interventions for individuals struggling with high-intensity digital stimuli and compulsive behaviors.

Evidence-Based Approach

We believe in the power of neuroscience and behavioral psychology. Our protocols are designed to help users stabilize their reward systems and regain cognitive control through focused advice and clinical reflections.

Professional Integrity

CFH is operated as an educational resource. We prioritize user privacy and data security above all else, ensuring a safe harbor for those on their journey toward behavioral freedom.
