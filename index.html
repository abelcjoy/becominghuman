Life Expectancy Analysis

Understanding your Life Expectancy is the first step toward intentional living. Our calculator uses global health statistics to provide an estimate of your remaining time based on your country of residence.

What is Conscious Time? Unlike a standard age calculator, we subtract the hours you spend sleeping. Since the average human spends 1/3 of their life asleep, your "conscious" time is significantly less than your total biological time.

Stoic Perspective: In the tradition of Memento Mori, this tool helps focus the mind on the present. By visualizing the finite nature of our hours, we can better prioritize what truly matters.

Our algorithm pulls from World Health Organization (WHO) data to ensure statistical accuracy in every calculation.

Neurobiological Optimization Protocol

01. Baseline Dopamine Restoration

To overcome addiction, you must manage your Dopamine Baseline. Spikes from substances or digital feeds cause an equal and opposite "crash" below baseline, creating cravings.

  • • Digital Fasting: Zero scrolling for the first 90 minutes of your day.
  • • Delay Gratification: Never combine two high-dopamine activities (e.g., eating + watching).

02. Parasympathetic Override

Anxiety and cravings are Sympathetic (Fight or Flight) responses. You can manually override this through the Vagus Nerve.

  • • The Physiological Sigh: Double inhale through nose, followed by a long, slow exhale through mouth. Repeat 3 times.
  • • Thermogenic Reset: 30 seconds of cold water exposure to trigger a natural dopamine release.

03. Circadian Precision

View sunlight within 30 minutes of waking. This triggers Cortisol release and sets a timer for Melatonin, regulating the brain's internal rhythm.

Specific Neutralization Modules

A. Digital Compulsion

The Gray Mode Hack: Set your device to grayscale. This removes the 'variable reward' of color, slashing interest by 40%.

Friction Layering: Delete apps, use only browser versions. Increased 'click friction' prevents limbic hijacking.

B. High-Arousal Loops

Environmental Cleansing: Cues are stored in space. Never use your device in the same place you sleep or relax.

The 15-Min Wave: Cravings are temporary neural spikes lasting ~15 mins. Use the 'Physiological Sigh' to survive the wave.

C. Chemical Reward

Glucose Blunting: 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice before meals blunts the insulin spike and subsequent brain crash.

Protein Priming: High protein intake at breakfast stabilized dopamine levels for the next 12 hours.

D. Substance Baseline

The 72-Hour Reset: It takes 3 days for primary withdrawal cues to weaken. Survival of the first 72 hours is the biological goal.

The Context Shift: Cravings often trigger at specific times/places. Change your physical environment for those specific hour windows.
