About

Our Mission

Clarity For Humans is an educational utility designed to foster mindfulness through data. We use World Health Organization (WHO) statistical life expectancy data combined with personal lifestyle factors like sleep to help individuals visualize their time.

Our goal is not to provoke anxiety, but to encourage intentionality. Every second counted here is a reminder of the precious nature of conscious life.
