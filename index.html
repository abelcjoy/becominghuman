Clarity for Humans (CFH) is just a simple place for anyone trying to cut through the noise of the modern world. We all know how easy it is to get stuck in a loop of scrolling and constant distractions. This site is here to help you slow down, reset, and find your focus again.

The goal is really simple: being in control of your own day. Instead of just reacting to every notification or urge, we want to help you get back to a point where you are the one making the choices.

We share daily thoughts and advice to keep you grounded. It takes time for the mind to clear up, but if you take it one day at a time, you will start seeing things differently. CFH is here to show that getting your clarity back is possible if you just stay consistent.

What is CFH?

CFH stands for Clarity for Humans. It is a project started because the digital world has become too loud and too addictive. Most of us are living in a fog of constant stimulation, and we have forgotten what it feels like to just be present and focused.

We do not believe in quick fixes or empty motivation. Instead, we look at how our habits actually affect us and how we can slowly untangle our brains from those "high dopamine" loops. It is about building a life that is not controlled by a screen.

Our Approach

The main idea here is consistent awareness. Every day, we post a few simple reminders or bits of advice to help you stay on track. Whether you are dealing with a specific habit or just feel overwhelmed by the internet, these posts are designed to give you a moment of clarity in your busy day.

Why we do this

We keep this site clean and simple because clarity should not be complicated. There are no tracking scripts, no personalized ads, and no complicated sign-ups. It is just a place for humans to get better.

How we handle your privacy

We care about your privacy as much as we care about our own. This page explains what little data we might see and what we do with it.

The Basics

Like almost every website, our hosting company keeps basic logs. This usually includes things like your IP address, what browser you use, and when you visited. We do not use this to identify you personally; it is just standard technical stuff that helps us keep the site running.

Google Ads

We use Google to show ads on our site. Google uses cookies (sometimes called DART cookies) to show ads based on your visit here and on other sites. If you do not like this, you can opt out in your Google account settings or by visiting the Google ad policy page.

Third Party Sites

Our privacy policy only covers what happens on our own site. If you click a link and go somewhere else, their rules apply instead. We recommend checking their privacy pages if you have concerns.

Acceptance

By using CFH, you are agreeing to how we handle things here. We keep it as simple and transparent as possible.
